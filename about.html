Come on in! Let me introduce myself.

Hey there! My name's Evelyn! I'm a Vancouver-based User Experience and Interface Designer, specializing in web-focused design and front-end development. For me, interface is not just about pixels and code; it's a living, breathing tool to forge connections with users, leaving a memorable impact.

I proudly hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Interactive Arts and Technology from Simon Fraser University, with a focus in UX design, a minor in computer science, alongside 8 months of co-op experience.

Beyond the tech and design realm, you'll find me geeking out over music, games, films, and doodling my heart out in the world of graphic art. These diverse interests aren't just hobbies; they contribute to my holistic design approach, infusing every project with creativity and a unique, multidimensional perspective.
