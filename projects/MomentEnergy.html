Overview

During my co-op at Moment Energy, I spearheaded the creation of animated infographics rooted in meticulous data and research. The design aimed to unravel the complexities of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS), the core offering of the company. The set of infographics was used for Moment Energy's website, trade shows, and pitch decks. By breaking down complex concepts like peak shaving and arbitrage, the visuals were designed to resonate with both tech enthusiasts and those less familiar with the technicalities. The project was driven by a commitment to showcasing the financial, sustainability, and environmental benefits of BESS across diverse customer segments.
