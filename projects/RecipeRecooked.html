Overview

Recipe Recooked is an mobile app designed to transform the cooking experience for young adults. This platform blends culinary guidance with user-friendly tools, catering to various skill levels and budgets. It offers a comprehensive collection of recipes, insightful cooking techniques, and nutritional information, making home cooking accessible and enjoyable for novices. Recipe Recooked is more than a recipe hub; it's a community connector, fostering a vibrant network of cooking enthusiasts. This app empowers users to explore and appreciate the art of cooking, creating a foundation for a lifelong culinary journey.

User Flow

Key Feature

Recipes

A practical recipe guide which home cooks share recipes with a standardized structure for clarity. Users can easily adjust serving sizes, and visual aids like step-by-step images assist in understanding. Each recipe concludes with user reviews, providing insights into the recipe's quality and success.

Key Feature

Grocery List

A convenient grocery list feature that automatically compiles and displays all ingredients required for selected recipes. Users benefit from streamlined meal planning and organized grocery lists. This functionality reduces the complexity of home cooking by automatically generating a list of necessary ingredients based on the user's chosen recipes.

Key Feature

Ingredient Detection Camera AI

The platform's feature simplifies creating recipe posts by identifying ingredients in images. It also makes recipe searches smoother by recognizing owned ingredients and generates grocery lists that exclude items already in the user's possession, making the experience more straightforward and personalized.
